The Roman Catholic Diocese of Cuddapah (Latin: Cuddapahen(sis)) is a diocese located in the city of Cuddapah in the Ecclesiastical province of Hyderabad in India.

Presently, there are about 51 parishes catering to 81,580 Catholics in the diocese.[1]

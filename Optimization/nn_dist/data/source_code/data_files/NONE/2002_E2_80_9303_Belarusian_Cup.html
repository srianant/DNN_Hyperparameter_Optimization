2002–03 Belarusian Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Belarusian Cup 2002–03 was the twelfth season of the Belarusian annual football cup competition. Contrary to the league season, it is conducted in a fall-spring rhythm. The first games were played on August 7, 2002. Winners of the Cup will qualified for the UEFA Cup first qualifying round.

First round[edit]

15 teams joined competition in this round. Five clubs from Second League were drawn against five clubs from First League. Another five First League teams advanced to the next round by drawing of lots. The rest of First and Second League teams (four from each) opted not to participate in this edition of the Cup. All games were played on 7 August 2002.

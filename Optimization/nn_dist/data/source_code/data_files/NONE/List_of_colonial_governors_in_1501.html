This is a list of the governors of colonies, protectorates, or other dependencies in 1501. Where applicable, native rulers are also listed.

Portugal[edit]

  • Portuguese Cape Verde
    • Santiago and Boa Vista – Rodrigo Afonso, Captain (1473–1505 and 1497–1505)
  • São Tomé Island – Fernão de Melo, Captain of São Tomé (c. 1499 – c. 1510)
  • Sofala – Sancho de Tovar, Captain-Major of Sofala (1501–1505)
  • Tangier – ?, Count of Tarouca, Governor of Tangier (1501–1508)
